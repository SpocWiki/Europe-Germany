
# [[Einbeck]]

> **Einbeck** (German pronunciation: [ˈaɪ̯nˌbɛk] ; Eastphalian: Aimbeck) is a town in the district Northeim, 
> in southern Lower Saxony, Germany, on the German Timber-Frame Road.
>

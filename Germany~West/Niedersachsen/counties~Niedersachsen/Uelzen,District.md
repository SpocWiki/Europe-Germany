## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Uelzen (German pronunciation: [ˈʏltsn̩] ) 
> is a district (Landkreis) in Lower Saxony, Germany. 
> It is bounded by (from the south and clockwise) the districts of 
> - Gifhorn, Celle, 
> - Heidekreis, 
> - Lüneburg and 
> - Lüchow-Dannenberg, 
> and by the state of Saxony-Anhalt (district of Altmarkkreis Salzwedel). 
> 
> The district capital is the town of [[Uelzen,District/cities~Uelzen/Uelzen,City|Uelzen]].
>

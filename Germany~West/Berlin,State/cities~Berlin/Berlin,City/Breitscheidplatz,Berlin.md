#is_/same_as :: [[_Standards/WikiData/WD~Breitscheidplatz,698370]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Breitscheidplatz** (German pronunciation: [bʁaɪtʃaɪtˌplats] ) is a major public square in the inner city of Berlin, Germany. 
> Together with the Kurfürstendamm boulevard, it marks the centre of former West Berlin and the present-day City West. 
> It is named after Rudolf Breitscheid.
>

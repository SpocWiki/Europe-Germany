#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Bergstraße_(district),7917]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Bergstraße (German pronunciation: [ˈbɛʁkˌʃtʁaːsə] , lit. 'Mountain Road') is a Kreis (district) in the Darmstadt region of southern Hesse, Germany. It is at the northern end of the Bergstraße route.
> 
> Neighboring districts are Groß-Gerau, Darmstadt-Dieburg, Odenwaldkreis, Rhein-Neckar-Kreis, the urban district Mannheim, the Rhein-Pfalz-Kreis, and the urban district of Worms. Kreis Bergstraße belongs both to the Rhine Neckar Area and the Rhein-Main Region.
>
